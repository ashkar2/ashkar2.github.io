MAC MILLER

I'm Ashka, a freshman at UIUC studying graphic design!

This page is about my favorite musical artist, Mac Miller...

Mac Miller began his debut as a hip hop music artist at the age of 18 with his mixtapes K.I.D.S & Best Day Ever. His top songs include Kool Aid and Frozen Pizza, Best Day Ever, Self Care, Come Back to Earth, and Good News. In 2018, due to struggle with substance, Mac Miller unfortunately passed away. Although Miller's career was short lived, his music explored a new perspective on the hip hop industry and was popular among many audiences.

While Mac Miller's albums are all unique in their own way, my personal favorite would be his K.I.D.S album - in particular The Spins. The Spins definitely gives me a nostalgic vibe reminding me about high school or hanging out with friends during the summer. The song has a stead drum beat with occasional keyboard riffs.
